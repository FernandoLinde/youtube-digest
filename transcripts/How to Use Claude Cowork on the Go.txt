The Co-work sessions are still hosted in a sandbox on your computer, meaning Claude still has the same access and protections. However, you can now kick off a Co-work session and then continue monitoring progress and providing approvals while out and about. Co-work developer Philipp Reisberg wrote, "It feels pretty magical to give Claude a mission on my computer and get occasional updates like creating reports from internal dashboards or finding me a better seat on my next flight.
