Aerospace & Defense:

*         Embraer (not rated) and Finnair have signed an agreement for up to 46 E195-E2 aircraft, comprising 18 firm orders, 16 options, and 12 purchase rights. The E195-E2 will replace Finnair’s older aircraft marking one of the largest investments in Finnair’s 102-year-old history. — Embraer <https://www.embraer.com/media-center/en/?mediatype=NEWS&detail=24788-Finnair-selects-Embraer-E195-E2%2C-ordering-up-to-46-aircraft> 

Our take: Positive read on Embraer. We estimate that the firm order of 18 aircraft could add approximately USD 624 million to Embraer’s firm order backlog, representing around 9% of its current commercial aviation backlog and 2.6% of the total backlog. If the 16 options are exercised, the total backlog impact would increase to approximately USD 1.17bn, equivalent to 17% of the commercial aviation backlog and 5% of the total backlog.
