Corporate access recap: Ahead of the curve 


Read the full document here >> <https://www.btgpactual.com/research/report-view/118848>  

Over the past six months, we have delivered investors with an extensive corporate access agenda, hosting 18 virtual and in person events with CEOs, CFOs, policymakers, pollsters, and industry leaders across Colombia and Argentina. These interactions provided investors with direct and unfiltered insights into strategy, regulation, capital allocation, voting dynamics, and macro risks at a time of elevated uncertainty in the region.

Our agenda combined high impact flagship events, including Colombia Virtual Week, a dedicated session with the CEO of AtlasIntel to discuss Colombia’s presidential elections, a timely discussion following Nicolás Maduro’s arrest and its regional implications, and Argentina O&G and Energy Week, alongside a continuous flow of targeted sessions across sectors.

Across interactions, one message stood out: access creates edge. Rising macro risks, improving corporate discipline, and a structurally attractive energy complex are redefining the investment landscape in the region. By connecting investors directly with decision-makers, our corporate access platform enabled early identification of inflection points, deeper conviction in investment theses, and a differentiated perspective on risks and opportunities not yet fully priced by the market.
