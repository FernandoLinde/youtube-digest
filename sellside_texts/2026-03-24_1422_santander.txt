Weekly Highlights:
*	U.S. chicken prices remain 10% down YoY, with slaughter volumes easing on lower weights.
*	Soybean crushing margins improved on the week, while biodiesel prices declined.
This Week’s Sector Data:
BZ Proteins:
*	Chicken: In 2025, domestic demand (implied) grew ~6% YoY, gaining share from other proteins, particularly beef.

*	Breeder flock increased, with higher productivity despite an older average age.


*	Hatchability was weak in 4Q, and placements remained low.


U.S. Proteins:
*	U.S. chicken prices remained down 10% YoY.

Agribusiness: 
*	Soybean crushing margins, including biodiesel, improved last week. That said, biodiesel prices remained soft.
