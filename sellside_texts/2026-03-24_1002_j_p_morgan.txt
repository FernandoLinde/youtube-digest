[In One Hour] Webinar with Close-Up International: GLP-1 in Brazil 
 	 
Hi Tork, 

You’re invited to join Joseph Giordano, JPM LatAm Healthcare Equity Analyst, for a conversation with Close-Up International’s Commercial Director, Carlos Aguiar. 

Close-Up International is a leading intelligence provider that has ~100k audited pharmacies in Brazil that enables high frequency data with TAM accuracy. Among the tracked data we highlight sell-in and sell-out data of GLP-1 drugs in Brazil. In this webinar, we will discuss the main trends across GLP-1 drugs in Brazil, semaglutide patent expiry, and the implications for pharma players.
