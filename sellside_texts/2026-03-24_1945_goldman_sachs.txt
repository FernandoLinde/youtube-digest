Meta Platforms Inc. (META): Framing Recent News Reports Against Our View of Strategic Focus Areas 
	
22 March 2026 | 6:40PM EDT | Research | Equity 	
	
	
In the past few weeks, multiple news reports have indicated that META management may take several potential actions: a) a large headcount restructuring; b) a reduction of investments in its Reality Labs segment; and c) a delay in the public release of the latest foundational models from the company’s Superintelligence Lab.

In this note, we examine all three potential actions and explore the resulting scope of 2026-2028 EPS upside if such initiatives were to be implemented.

We also address what we see as the key investor debate currently facing META: the duration and/or return on invested capital across both META's AI compute & non-Family Apps spending in the coming years (a theme that has also been an area of focus for the other major US hyperscalers under our coverage - GOOGL & AMZN).

We reiterate our Buy rating & our 12-month price target of $835.
