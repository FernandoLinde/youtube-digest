PRIO (PRIO3.SA): First Take: Wahoo's first well producing 12kbpd; guidance of 40kbpd maintained 
	
23 March 2026 | 10:03AM BRT | Research | Equity 	
	
	
On March 23th, PRIO announced that the first producing well of Wahoo reached a stabilized production of 12kbpd (above our original estimate of 10kbpd average production per well). Nevertheless, PRIO reiterated it expects production to reach 40kbpd by the end of April (GSe 38kbpd in May and 40kbpd in June).

Sensitivities. Our analysis suggests every 8kbpd additional production in Wahoo (or additional 2kpbd per well, given four producing wells) could translate into a c.+1 p.p. upside to FCFy for 26/27E, all else held equal. 

We remain Buy-rated on what we view as a reasonable valuation (2026E FCFy of ~20% assuming average oil prices of USD 80/bbl), coupled with strong organic production growth in the near term and solid track record in creating value through inorganic opportunities.
