Assunto: Sabesp's reservoirs



Dear friends,

We’re approaching the end of the rainy season.

Sabesp considers reaching over 40% (by the end of March) in the Cantareira system to be safe for the year. Cantareira is currently at 44%.

They believe the ideal situation would be for the system to stay above 50% (which won’t happen, as there is only one week left).

The most likely scenario is that the ongoing nighttime pump restrictions remain in place for the remainder of the year, especially if El Niño is confirmed for the winter. The worst-case scenario, though, can be disregarded for 2026.
