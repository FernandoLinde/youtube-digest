Mexico: Firm Jan Retail Sales and Non-Financial Services Activity (Ramos) 
	
23 March 2026 | 8:24AM EDT | Research | Economics 	
	
	
Bottom-Line: Retail sales recorded a solid 1.0% mom sa in Jan, above consensus for a milder 0.2% increase. Non-financial services sequential activity rose 0.3% mom sa, partially offsetting the Dec decline. The retail sales carry for 1Q2026 is tracking at 1.4% qoq sa, and for services at 0% qoq sa. Sequential real wage growth was positive in both retail (1.9% mom sa; 7.0% yoy sa) and services (0.4% mom sa; 2.6% yoy sa), with employment growth firmer in retail (0.4% mom sa; 1.3% yoy sa) and weak in services (-0.1% mom sa; -1.1% yoy sa).

Going forward, household consumption of goods and services is likely to continue to face headwinds from lingering domestic and external uncertainty. On the positive side, household spending is likely to benefit from generous minimum wage increases, a still resilient labor market backdrop, and solid though moderating real wage growth.

KEY NUMBERS (Jan):

Retail Sales: 5.0% yoy (1.0% mom sa; 4.7% yoy sa) vs. consensus forecast at 3.4% yoy (+0.2% mom sa); GS at +0.2% mom sa; prior revised 4.3% yoy (0% mom sa).

Private Non-Financial Services: -0.7% yoy sa (0.3% mom sa); prior 1.0% yoy sa (-0.7% mom sa).

DETAILS:

1. 

Retail sales rose a solid 1.0% mom sa in Jan, well above the Bloomberg consensus for 0.2% mom sa and firming from the flat mom sa Dec print (revised from -0.1% mom sa).

2. 

On an annual basis, retail sales rose 5.0% yoy in Jan, above the 3.4% yoy Bloomberg consensus. The seasonal and calendar-adjusted series show retail sales up 4.7% yoy sa, vs. 4.2% yoy in Dec.

3. 

The statistical carry for 1Q2026 is at a high 1.4% qoq sa (3.1% for 2026).

4. 

Employment in the retail sector rose 1.3% yoy sa (0.4% mom sa) in Jan and average real wages rose 7.0% yoy sa (1.9% mom sa).

5. 

Non-Financial Services rose 0.3% mom sa in Jan, rebounding from the -0.7% mom sa Dec print (unrevised at one decimal point).

6. 

On an annual basis, private non-financial services activity recorded a -0.7% yoy sa variation (vs. 1.0% yoy sa in Dec).

7. 

The statistical carry for 1Q2026 services activity sequential growth stands at 0.04% qoq sa; with carry for 2026 at also 0%.

8. 

Services sector employment declined 0.1% mom sa in Jan but real wages rose 0.4% mom sa (2.6% yoy sa).
