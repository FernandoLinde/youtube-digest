In case you missed - some of our report pieces so far

As we reach the end of Q1, one key takeaway stands out: it has been a particularly busy start to the year. In January, we saw a strong wave of equity inflows into Brazil, largely passive in nature, which drove outperformance among large caps and blue chips, supported by their higher index weights and liquidity. In February, investor focus shifted to AI-related disruption themes across selected names. More recently, in March, markets have been impacted by escalating conflicts in the Middle East, alongside rising oil prices and a broad risk-off move across both equity and fixed income globally. Against this backdrop, we have published several reports spanning thematic and long-term views, as well as shorter-term trading calls and scenario-based first takes, which we compile below.
