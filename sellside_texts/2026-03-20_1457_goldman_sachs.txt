Latin America Energy: Oil: What to Buy in a mid cycle oil price environment in the USD70's range? PBR, PRIO, Vista stand out 
	
20 March 2026 | 2:51PM BRT | Research | Equity 	
	
	
Oil prices soared to USD107/bbl mainly due to the ongoing conflict in the middle east. However, the market seems to be pricing in a scenario where the conflict is likely short-lived (2y forward Brent @ ~USD75/bbl, significantly below spot prices). 

Historically, equities are usually priced based on the market's view on mid-cycle oil prices. With this in mind, we present an assessment of what is likely priced in, as well as what could be the potential implications of the current environment on dividends and any boost to oil production across our coverage.
