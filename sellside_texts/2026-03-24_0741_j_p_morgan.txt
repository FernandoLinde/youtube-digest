Colombia 


Post-Primary Boost: Paloma Valencia Is Climbing in Colombia’s Presidential Polls. Following the March 8 primaries that clarified candidacies, Paloma Valencia (Uribismo) has gained momentum and, for the first time in a CNC poll, moved into second place ahead of Abelardo de la Espriella. The latest results show the continuity candidate leading with 34.5%, Valencia at 22.2%, and De la Espriella at 15.4%, while Sergio Fajardo and Claudia López trail below 4%.
