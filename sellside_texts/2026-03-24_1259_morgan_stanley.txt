March 20, 2026

	
OEMs in the U.S. accelerate toward autonomy, with TSLA's robotaxi flywheel and RIVN x UBER deal signaling a push to unlock software and fleet-based monetization. However, weaker EV demand, lower global production forecasts, and rising commodity inflation continue to temper near-term fundamentals.
