Argentina 


Argentina’s Trade Surplus Narrows to USD $788 Million in February, a Seven‑Month Low. Argentina’s trade surplus fell to USD $788 million in February, the lowest since July, as exports contracted and imports rebounded after four consecutive monthly declines. INDEC reported exports of USD $5.962 billion for the month, down 2.9% year over year, contributing to the slimmer surplus.

Peru 


SNMPE Warns Congressional Changes to General Mining Law Threaten Formal Mining in Peru. Peru’s National Society of Mining, Oil and Energy (SNMPE) cautioned that amendments approved by Congress’s Energy and Mines Committee to the General Mining Law endanger formal mining. The industry group argues the bill effectively undermines the mining concession regime, shortening timelines to bring projects online and would contract investment and render the USD $63 billion project pipeline unviable in the coming years.
