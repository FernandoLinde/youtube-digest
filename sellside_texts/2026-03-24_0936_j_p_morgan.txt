We wanted to flag to you the following webinar on Agentic Commerce with JD Sports that will be hosted by our EU General Retail and Luxury & Sporting Goods team. You can also read the following note from the team for reference (Change Agents <https://jpmorgan.email.streetcontxt.net/platform/al?a=12832578&ad=4071433062&h=SsLaoud&sig=UPsIJ8Nj4dITcj55ybsvEGxUf3e&v=2&url=https://markets.jpmorgan.com/research/email/scx/-lh0svb6/GPS-5176181-0/65b060bd-9e62-4750-bcab-6f63e251850c> ) 

The research team will host JD Sports Chief Technology & Transformation Office, Jetan Chowk and Group Director of Product, Antonia Hansen. The JD Sports team will discuss the group’s recent announcement that it will be enabling US customers to purchase goods from JD directly through AI agents (without leaving the agents’ apps) in the coming months. It is an opportunity to better understand key considerations for retailers as they prepare for a potential consumer shift towards agentic commerce.
