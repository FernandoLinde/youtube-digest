This sixth edition of our Argentina Country Handbook is a concise primer and reference on the country. Published for 2026 after the 2025 midterm elections, it reflects an increasingly optimistic outlook as reforms and investment move to center stage. We have updated all key figures, tables, and indicators, and added sections on contingent macroeconomic and political developments. This long-standing Economics & Equity Strategy collaboration is intended to be a go-to reference for investors thinking about investing in Argentina, regardless of whether they are already an investor in the country or are looking at it for the first time. It covers the economy, politics, financial markets, social development, and key sectors.
