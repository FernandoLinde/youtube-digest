*	Cyrela CFO flags potential corporate tower sale in interview - Metro Quadrado <https://jpmorgan.email.streetcontxt.net/platform/al?a=12831559&ad=4070836581&h=-yQUw7w&sig=2mplAERKKsDp1Gk5ZyGAgwbzKqT&v=2&url=https://metroquadrado.com/comercial/venda-de-predio-locado-para-o-nubank-esta-no-radar-da-cyrela/> . Cyrela’s (OW-rated) CFO Miguel Mickelberg said the 23‑story building (17 floors leased to Nubank, 6 for Cyrela’s new HQ) is unlikely to remain on the balance sheet for long because stabilized office yields tend to sit below the company’s ROE, though high rates mean “no rush” to sell. One option is to sell only the 17 Nubank floors and retain the 6 Cyrela-occupied floors; the tower is due for completion in 2H26. Regarding 2026, management sees “lower chances” of materially accelerating launches in 2026 and sees sales speed at a still “healthy” level with some normalization ahead from an unusually high level in 2025. 
*	Melnick pivots to MCMV in Porto Alegre to reignite growth - Metro Quadrado <https://jpmorgan.email.streetcontxt.net/platform/al?a=12831559&ad=4070836581&h=-qm2Ljt&sig=2POiBUKYM5vO8f4V4gLkF8fbP2g&v=2&url=https://metroquadrado.com/residencial/melnick-muda-rota-para-pegar-carona-no-avanco-do-mcmv-em-porto-alegre/> . Melnick (MELK, NC) will redirect land bank and focus to the MCMV program, with its Open vertical targeting R$350mn in launches in 2026 (+80% yoy), primarily in Faixa 3 and Faixa 4, after floods and weaker demographics/interest rates eroded mid/high-end demand. The company is renegotiating land deals, with up to 80% discounts in swaps, arguing higher unit volumes at MCMV will offset price cuts. Company’s landbank is at R$4.4bn, with ~21% (~R$1.5bn) earmarked for Open. The pivot seeks to address pent-up affordable-housing demand supported by state “Porta de Entrada” subsidies and pending federal adjustments to income/price caps, while tackling analyst concerns about Melnick’s growth. 
*	Tecnisa withdraws launch and margin guidance for Jardim das Perdizes – Press Release <https://jpmorgan.email.streetcontxt.net/platform/al?a=12831559&ad=4070836581&h=IAqhO5p&sig=N7U4DAR38kr1khSm7mZ0Qyu1TGm&v=2&url=https://www.rad.cvm.gov.br/ENET/frmExibirArquivoIPEExterno.aspx?NumeroProtocoloEntrega%3D1492701> .
