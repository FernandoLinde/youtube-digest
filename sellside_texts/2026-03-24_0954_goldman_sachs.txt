Brazil Energy: Fuel distribution: February market share data shows some recovery for largest players; March could be better 
	
23 March 2026 | 9:50AM BRT | Research | Equity 	
	
	
ANP (Brazil oil and gas regulator) released data for fuels sales volumes for February. All in all, data indicates the largest distributors were able to recovery some market share lost in January (although still lags behind the share seen in December). However, given i) PBR has been charging diesel prices meaningfully below international benchmarks, and ii) news flow indicates a tighter S&D in March, we expect market share data for the next couple of months to show clearer improvements for the largest distributors in our coverage. 

We remain optimistic in our fuel distribution coverage and reiterate our relative preference for Vibra <https://publishing.gs.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fpublishing.gs.com%2FtrustedLink%3Fopentoken=T1RLAQIuW2PHfNa9kd-lcxZsDS6sLDIWkRAw-TGwUF9PN2qDKFCOlbnNAACwRee_ykbA-plTvHh7Vu-Nc9avvki7JfA4TEkETVZG_4GOQnLBlGi11cOi80dsEaA_0ubn3GSEwFfNhYPqfLnzUfta_i4LFI5C_pgLWrAqZbVnryJn07rT5v7f12DdkfZ4QnAZkUo5wiXmseaYAJ4y2-ZyKJJxVYrZYQJymnknps7YfieckEn_XjFMVXPgy0yu-ctpwGlYfHvgH595gD7mYX4j-MHYHJ62AcppctlF8zU*%26id=188d8ed8-43c6-4049-bf40-29315df39d54%26utm_medium=pa/1/0100019d1ac2a4df-50fff654-15ee-4c5f-a5a3-d81187caee7a-000000/s5OLP16UWfyPf_vGTfGyoCOzxQxterFylIntGywpOlQ=449> , essentially a pure-play in fuel distribution, trading at reasonable valuation (c.11-9x P/Adj. net income 2026-27E).
