Brazil Utilities: Updating our views on Eneva and Copel post capacity auction. TPs raised 
	
22 March 2026 | 6:04PM BRT | Research | Equity 	
	
	
We are increasing our price target for Eneva to BRL 30/sh (from BRL 25/sh) and Copel to BRL 17/sh (from BRL 14/sh). In this report, we update our views on Eneva and Copel following the conclusion of the Capacity Reserve Auction. As discussed in our LRCAP report <https://publishing.gs.com/CL0/https:%2F%2Fpublishing.gs.com%2FtrustedLink%3Fopentoken=T1RLAQLXUASXlKsxeHauwHILUysHkihvYRBLF-rsgovJ6thoVsgd85MnAACwmVPWJQPYLkRDQv9e3NmvWQQFEYTMEQhvh4uJ4PQHn32eKunNtl7f2OZA4C62VqmB0g7-MiRi7Z67CAIK5ak0nTCpJFGUrwXi4qphHXMhqVsBDQrl10ugy1IkvZeoRTFgk0Bo4Rw0bS8C3Gm-l3thcJDIjmv0iymrqcnVveq9BirJ482IncRQJ9Qqv0FbyT4TiRPIKTpKtdkIZxC7UgJsrrvTnzhah4vViQcJ09FO6-U*%26id=84dd9a1e-c7a7-4a3b-bc99-1ab3910734d0%26utm_medium=pa/1/0100019d17609cc0-71f87f3d-5d40-42dc-b0ee-86343d2360e6-000000/oP6rxaFOTd5t7UeeC0XQE2CxHXJ3pvpjBEudM4_EhCM=449> , Eneva exceeded our expectations by contracting not only its expired/expiring assets (except for Termofortaleza) and Celse 2 greenfield, in line with our base case scenario, but also contracting other two greenfield projects - Ceara (~1.1GW) and Sudeste (~1GW) hubs - at attractive prices, while Copel was also a highlight contracting both its Foz do Areia and Segredo hydro plants' expansion (~1.9GW hired capacity) at cap. prices.
