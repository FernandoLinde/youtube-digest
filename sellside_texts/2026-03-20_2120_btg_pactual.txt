Dear clients,

We are pleased to announce the addition of new features to our Strategy Dashboard <https://best.pactual.net/btg10sim.com> . The platform now includes buyback and insider activity data for all companies listed on B3. At the top of the page, you will find the top 10 companies active in buybacks, with the option to toggle between % of market cap and total financial amount (see screenshot below). Further down the page, you can filter the data by specific periods and select individual companies to explore their buyback activity in more detail. In the “Insiders” tab, you will find similar information on insider buying and selling for companies listed on B3. The platform updates automatically in real time as soon as a company files a new document with the CVM.

 

Screenshot of Buyback tab in our Strategy Dashboard

 <https://salesforce-mailing-distribution-prod.s3.sa-east-1.amazonaws.com/Mailing%20Distribution/assets/2026-03-19/1773963613213.png> 

We have also added a “Strategy Cheat Sheet” section, a comprehensive table designed to help investors quickly distill the key high-frequency data points driving markets. In this section, you can track movements across Brazilian and U.S. nominal and real interest rates, forward expectations for inflation, GDP, and FX, as well as key commodities and equity indices, among other indicators. The goal is to provide a centralized and intuitive snapshot of the macro landscape, helping investors monitor trends and identify potential inflection points more efficiently.
